What to Look for When Buying a Laptop or PC

By Tiger Tech | April 27, 2025

Choosing the right laptop or PC can be challenging with so many options available. Whether you need a machine for gaming, work, or casual browsing, here’s what you should focus on:

Important Features to Consider

  • Processor (CPU): For regular use, Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 is enough. For gaming and heavy work, go for Core i7/i9 or Ryzen 7/9.
  • RAM: At least 8GB is recommended for casual users, and 16GB+ for gamers, designers, and programmers.
  • Storage: SSDs are much faster than HDDs. A 512GB SSD is ideal, and some users might need a combination of SSD + HDD.
  • Graphics Card (GPU): Gamers and video editors need dedicated GPUs like NVIDIA RTX 3060 or better. Casual users can stick with integrated graphics.
  • Battery Life (for Laptops): Choose models offering at least 8 hours of real-world usage if portability matters.
  • Display: A Full HD (1920x1080) display is the minimum. Higher refresh rates (120Hz+) are better for gaming.
  • Build Quality: Lightweight aluminum designs are durable. Also, check for MIL-STD-810G certifications if ruggedness is important.

Other Factors to Think About

- Keyboard comfort and trackpad responsiveness.
- Webcam quality for video calls (important in remote work/study).
- Number and type of ports (USB-C, HDMI, SD Card Readers).
- Upgradability of RAM and Storage.

Conclusion

Before you buy, list your top needs and match them against the specs. A little extra research ensures you won’t regret your laptop or PC choice!
